## üé¨ Recording Tips

### Before Recording:
1. **Clear browser cache** - Fresh session for loading screen
2. **Full screen browser** (F11) - No toolbars
3. **Close other tabs** - Better performance
4. **Start on http://localhost:3000** in incognito mode
5. **Practice the flow 2-3 times** - Smooth transitions

### During Recording:
- **Speak clearly and confidently**
- **Move mouse slowly** when showing features
- **Pause 1-2 seconds** between major sections
- **Use screen recorder**: OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Record at 1080p minimum**

### Recommended Flow:
```
1. Open localhost:3000 (incognito)
```
